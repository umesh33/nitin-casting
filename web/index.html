Introduction

Nitin Alloys Global Ltd. was started in February 1998, near Silvassa, The Union Territory of Dadra & Nagar Haveli, 150kms away from Mumbai. We manufacture all grades of Heat, Wear & Corrosion Resistant Steels by Centrifugal, Sand Casting, Shell Molding & Investment Casting methods. Nitin Alloys Global Ltd. is ISO 9001:2008 certified by M/s. Bureau Veritas and AD 2000 Merkblatt WO (PED) by TUV Rheinland for supplying high pressure equipments. We cater to the ever increasing demand of steel castings from 5gm to 2 metric tons single piece on a global level.
